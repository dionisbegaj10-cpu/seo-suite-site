Studio Fyrnys

Studio Fyrnys designs interiors that hold up to daily life without losing their sense of craft. Whether the client is a homeowner or a business, the studio digs into the specifics of each project — how the space will actually be used, what the client is really going for — before anything gets drawn up. That close collaboration is what makes each space feel distinct rather than generic, and genuinely built around the people who'll live or work in it.
