Client

KFO Bergschneider

Tucked in the heart of Frankfurt, KFO Bergschneider offers orthodontic care for kids, teens, and adults alike, led by Dr. Felix Bergschneider. The practice pairs newer treatment methods — Invisalign, lingual braces, early intervention for younger patients — with something a bit more old-fashioned: actually tailoring the plan to the person in the chair. The result is a healthy, natural-looking smile, built in a space that doesn't feel clinical or rushed.
