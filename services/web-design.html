Common questions

How long does a typical website take to build?

A focused marketing site usually takes 3-5 weeks from kickoff to launch. Larger builds with eCommerce, custom functionality or a headless CMS run longer — we'll give you a realistic timeline once we know the scope.

Can you redesign an existing site instead of building from scratch?

Yes — a lot of our work is redesigning sites that have grown messy over time. We'll assess what's worth keeping (content, SEO equity, existing structure) before deciding whether a rebuild or a redesign makes more sense.

Do you build on WordPress, or something else?

It depends on the project. WordPress is a strong fit for content-heavy sites that need frequent updates; Webflow suits design-led builds; a headless setup makes sense when performance and custom functionality matter most. We'll recommend the platform that fits your actual needs, not just the one we default to.
