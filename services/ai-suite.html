AI Suite

SEO AI Suite is our own software — an AI-powered SEO platform with 20+ built-in skills.

It connects to Google Search Console and the AI model of your choice to plan content, optimize pages, audit sites, track rankings and monitor competitors, all from one dashboard.

Instead of juggling a dozen disconnected SEO tools, everything lives in one place — built to keep your site visible in both traditional search and the AI assistants people increasingly ask for recommendations.

SEO AI Suite was built because we were tired of stitching together five different tools to run one SEO strategy — a keyword tool here, a rank tracker there, a separate audit tool, a separate content planner. Everything now lives in one dashboard connected directly to your Search Console data, so recommendations are based on how your site is actually performing, not generic industry benchmarks.

What's included

  • Content planning and topic gap analysis
  • On-page optimization recommendations per URL
  • Automated technical SEO audits
  • Rank tracking across search engines and AI assistants
  • Competitor monitoring and benchmarking
  • Direct Google Search Console integration

Common questions

Is this a replacement for tools like Ahrefs or SEMrush?

For most of what small and mid-size businesses actually use those tools for — rank tracking, audits, content planning — yes. Where AI Suite differs is that it connects directly to your own Search Console data and gives AI-generated recommendations specific to your site, rather than generic keyword databases.

Which AI model does it use?

You choose — SEO AI Suite connects to the AI model of your choice rather than locking you into one provider, so you're not dependent on a single company's roadmap or pricing changes.

Do I need technical knowledge to use it?

No — it's built for business owners and marketers, not developers. The 20+ skills run through a guided dashboard, not a command line or raw data export.
