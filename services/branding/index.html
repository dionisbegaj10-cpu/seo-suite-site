Branding

We build brands from the ground up — naming, tone of voice, visual identity, art direction and copywriting.

Every brand strategy is designed to be distinctive, consistent and built to last: a foundation strong enough that every future website, campaign and piece of content reinforces the same identity instead of starting from scratch.

Good branding isn't decoration — it's the reason people recognize and trust you before they've read a single word.

A brand isn't a logo — it's the accumulated impression every touchpoint leaves, from the words on your homepage to the tone of a customer support email. We start by defining what a business actually stands for and who it's actually for, then build the naming, voice and visual system around that, so every future piece of content has a foundation to build on instead of starting from a blank page each time.

What's included

  • Naming and brand strategy
  • Tone of voice and messaging guidelines
  • Visual identity: logo, color, typography systems
  • Art direction for photography and campaigns
  • Brand guidelines documentation for internal and external use
  • Copywriting aligned to the brand voice across channels

Common questions

We already have a logo — do we need full branding?

A logo is one piece of a brand, not the whole thing. If your visual identity, tone of voice and messaging feel inconsistent across your website, social and marketing, that inconsistency is what full branding work fixes — sometimes that means keeping the existing logo and building the system around it.

How long does a branding project take?

A focused identity project (naming, visual system, guidelines) typically runs 4-6 weeks. Projects that include extensive market research or multiple stakeholder rounds take longer — we'll scope the actual timeline based on what's involved.

Do you handle brand rollout across our existing website and materials?

We can, either as part of the branding engagement or as a follow-on project once the identity is finalized — that's often paired with our web design or content work so the new brand shows up consistently everywhere.
