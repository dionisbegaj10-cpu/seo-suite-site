Hosting

Fast, secure, fully managed hosting — SSL, daily backups, updates and monitoring included.

Your website stays online, up to date and performing at its best while we handle the technical side: server maintenance, security patches, uptime monitoring and backups, so a technical issue never becomes your problem to solve at 2am.

One less vendor to manage, one less thing that can silently break — hosting handled by the same team that built the site.

Hosting is the part of a website nobody thinks about until it breaks, which is exactly why we handle it the way we do: proactively, not reactively. Every site we host gets the same baseline — SSL, automated daily backups, security patching and uptime monitoring — regardless of plan size, because a small business site going down costs just as much stress as a large one, even if the traffic numbers differ.

What's included

  • SSL certificates, issued and auto-renewed
  • Automated daily backups with easy restore
  • Server and platform security patching
  • 24/7 uptime monitoring with alerts
  • CDN setup for faster global load times
  • Direct support from the team that built your site

Common questions

What happens if my site goes down?

Uptime monitoring alerts us immediately, and since we built and host the site, we're not waiting on a third-party host's support queue to diagnose the issue — we already know the stack.

Can I migrate my existing site to your hosting?

Yes — we handle the migration, including DNS cutover, so there's no downtime during the switch. We'll walk you through the process and timeline before starting.

Do you host sites you didn't build?

In most cases, yes, though we'll do a quick technical review first to make sure the site is in a state we can reliably maintain and secure.
